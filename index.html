What this calculator does

This calculator calculates rubber and plastic oil loops for Satisfactory.
If you do not want to produce fuel, and instead want to produce a different heavy oil residue product, or just heavy oil residue:
Enter the equivelant amount of fuel.

What is an oil loop?

An oil loop, or a plastic/rubber/fuel loop is a way of making plastics and rubbers in Satisfactory.
It is the most efficient way to do so, as it resuses polymer resin byproducts when making heavy oil residue,
and uses the heavy oil residue to make fuel, which is used with the recycled rubber and recycled plastic recipes to maximise output.
